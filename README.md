### Moody Tunes
Welcome!
This is a quick little way to introduce other people to your playlists. It presents you with sliders where you can describe what type of music you're in the mood for, and it will generate three playlists that best match the input you gave on the sliders.

No AI, just crunching and comparing numbers.

### Example - Click [here](https://ccaitlingo.github.io/moody-tunes/)!
* My website: https://ccaitlingo.github.io/moody-tunes/
* My spotify: [Caitlin G.](https://open.spotify.com/user/swglws3e2wqtmzlsdndg9zfi2?si=4d8a7a7029394c71)

### How to use
1. Clone the repo
2. Fill out inputs.csv
3. Run `playlist_loader.py`. This will load inputs.csv into a Javascript-friendly format to incorporate your playlists into the web app.
4. Push your changes
5. Go to https://<your_github_username>.github.io/moody-tunes/

### Have fun!

When cloning the repo, please provide the link to your website and your spotify profile, so other people can check out your music taste. Cheers 😊
